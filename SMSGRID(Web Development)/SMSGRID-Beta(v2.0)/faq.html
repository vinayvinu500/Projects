FAQ

Frequently asked question

Why am I unable to send messages after 8:45 PM or before 9 AM?

Transactional messages such as OTPs and order updates can be delivered 24x7.

By default, you are configured a promotional account. As per TRAI regulations, promotional SMS can be delivered only from 9am to 9pm.

If you would like to send transactional SMS, contact our regional manager or email us at leads@eugence.com to change your account to a transactional account.

Why am I unable to send messages to DND numbers through promotional route?

SMSGrid ensures you comply with TRAI regulations and automatically reject all the DND numbers from your recipients.

If you are configured to promotional route then as per TRAI regulations, promotional SMS cannot be delivered to numbers registered in the National Do-Not-Disturb (DND) registry.

Transactional messages such as OTPs and order alerts can be sent to all numbers, including the ones registered in DND.

What are sender id format and approval time?

Sender Id can only be of 6 letters and no numbers are allowed.

Once the request id raised we try to approve sender in 2-4 working hours.

Sender Id can be sent for approval from 10AM to 5:30PM from Monday to Friday and 10AM to 2PM on Saturday.

Why am I unable to import excel sheet?

Excel sheet can only be into comma seperated value (.csv) format.

What is the format for excel sheet for Custom SMS?

Excel sheet can only be into comma seperated value (.csv) format.

First column (i.e. Column A) should contains only mobile nos whereas rest of the columns can contain the required data which has to be sent.
